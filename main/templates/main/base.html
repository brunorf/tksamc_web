TKSA-MC

Tanford-Kirkwood Surface Accessibility - Monte Carlo

The TKSA-MC method indicates the residues which contribute to destabilizing the protein native state. The algorithm calculates the protein electrostatic energy taking into account the contribution of each residue with polar charged side chain.
